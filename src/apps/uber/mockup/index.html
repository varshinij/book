The CookIt service helps users get people who can cook a meal they feel like eating. Typically we provide two types of services:

  • Standard: brings a chef to you're home to cook a meal for you/a group
  • Premium: brings a specialty chef w/ ingredients to you're home to cook a meal

​


  • Who can use the services?

    Anyone can use this service and they would have to register with a valid credit/debit card.

  • Who can join as the service providers?

    Anyone with a valid identification/background check

  • What are the main data attributes of a service provider (e.g., name, stars, location)?
    • Teach you how to cook
    • Types of food
    • Ratings
    • Locations
  • Which city will your startup initially launch?

    San Francisco

  • What is the price for a customer to pay for a service? What are the factors determining the price?
    • Modifier for web traffic
    • Ingredients
  • How much can a service provider earn?
    Based off of
    • Time
    • Quality
    • Quantity
