What is Critique?

Critique is a public platform for discussing about your favourite boooks.You can chat with anyone and hence not restricted to just your friends. If you are a voracious reader or not this is a great tool to let your creativty flow. You can talk about your favourite character, the story line, what you did not understand and much more!

You can serach for books and if you dont find one then you can add it and start conversing.Each chat room is unique to a particular book. You can see names and pictures of other users as they participate in the chat.You can as long as you want but each chat has a limitation on number of words which is 75. This is to ensure your chat is not too long for the other person to read.

Go to the App!
How many concurrent users?
Critique is a very user friendly application and hence to provide the best user experience, we limit the maximum concurrent users to 40. This is done so that you can talk for a long time with the same set of users and hence not burdened by too many people.We understand that too much of collaboration can also cause confusion!
What skills do you need?
All you need is love for books and the desire to talk about it.
Just for Fun?
Learning different perspectives of the same story helps your mind to think out of the box.This is a fun application but also helps you think more creatively.If there are too many ideas floating around, maybe you can write a book too!
How do you communicate?
You know who is participating in the chat from their profile picture and their name that appears when you hover over their chat. Each book has a separate chat room to provide uniqueness.You can see others recent posts from their chats in that particular rrom.
As an Admin
You can see all the users, their chat history, searches and books that they have added
